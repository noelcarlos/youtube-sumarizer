The video begins by introducing Dr. Valter Longo's ProLon diet, which has been extensively studied in both animals and humans. This diet is designed to provide the benefits of fasting without complete food deprivation.

*   **Composition:** Plant-based, roughly 750 calories per day on average.
*   **Frequency:** Typically repeated once a month for several months.
*   **Mechanism:**
    *   Mimics fasting at the cellular level.
    *   Activates repair pathways within the body.
    *   Clears out old and damaged immune cells.
    *   Kicks stem cells into gear, promoting the creation of new cells.
    *   Initiates a "cleanup and regeneration process," termed a "metabolic reset."
    *   Triggers autophagy (cell cleanup) and reactivates stem cells in multiple tissues, including the immune system.
    *   Promotes rejuvenation of the pancreas and liver.
*   **Observed Benefits:**
    *   Improved metabolic markers: decreases in fasting glucose, IGF-1, blood pressure, and cholesterol.
    *   Better insulin sensitivity.
    *   Reduced markers of inflammation.
    *   In animal models, led to stem cell regeneration and autophagy.
    *   In both mice and human pilot studies, cycles of the diet led to a reduction of old, damaged immune cells and a rebound in new immune cell production, effectively an "immune system reboot."
*   **Duration of Effects:** Even after a single 5-day cycle, some metabolic improvements, such as reduced IGF-1 and improved glucose control, persisted for weeks and even months.
